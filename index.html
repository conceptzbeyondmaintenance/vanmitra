The Challenges

  • Soil quality declined due to years of chemical farming practices and lack of proper care.
  • Unemployment and poverty in tribal communities.
  • Environmental degradation and harmful agricultural practices.

Our Solutions

  • Restore soil with organic techniques like vermicomposting and biogas.
  • Plant 500,000 Moringa trees to boost green cover.
  • Train farmers in sustainable practices and provide year-round employment.

Results So Far

In six months, we've harvested 400 kg of Moong, grown fodder, and planted 20,000 Moringa trees, now ready for harvest. Our work has rejuvenated the ecosystem, bringing birds, butterflies, and small animals to the previously barren land, boosting regional biodiversity

Why Weʼre Unique

Our approach blends agro forestry, community empowerment & a self-sustaining ecosystem powered by local resources, promoting environmental health, economic growth, and sustainability while enhancing ecological balance & resilience for the community.
