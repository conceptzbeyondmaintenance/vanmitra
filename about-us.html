About Us

About Van Mitra

At VanMitra, we believe the health of people and the health of the planet are inseparable. VanMitra – Friends of Forest is dedicated to working in harmony with nature to rejuvenate degraded lands, empower tribal communities and farmers, and create a model of sustainable agroforestry.

With a clear vision to transform so-called “barren” lands into thriving, productive farms, we are cultivating food that is pure, wholesome, and full of life. Our 12-acre farm in Kutushol, Jharkhand, stands as a living example of this transformation—showcasing what’s possible when people and nature grow together.

Founders

The venture is led by a dynamic team of three passionate individuals from diverse yet complementary backgrounds. Together they are spearheading a movement rooted in innovation, ecological restoration, and community empowerment.

Rahul Lodha

An IIT Kharagpur graduate (2004) with 17 years in software development, Rahul returned to Chakulia, Jharkhand in 2021, inspired by Mother Nature’s call. Founder of Urveera, he works on regenerative development through permaculture, natural farming, elephant conservation, natural building, and education. His mission is to restore ecosystems while empowering farmers and fostering harmony between people and nature.

Lalit Agarwal

Born in Kolkata, Lalit holds a Commerce degree and a deep commitment to sustainability and spirituality. As Founder Trustee of Gram Samriddhi Foundation, he has enabled 7,000+ farmers to adopt sustainable agriculture, created 500+ rain water harvesting ponds, & planted 600,000+ trees. His journey reflects dedication to rural prosperity, ecological balance, and empowering communities through regenerative, nature-aligned solutions.

Narotam Badgotra

An MBA with 20+ years in branding and marketing, Narotam provides honest, sustainable solutions to diverse clients while pursuing his vision for agroforestry. He is passionate about building regenerative farming models that rejuvenate ecosystems, empower rural communities, and promote healthier living. Combining creativity, strategy, and sustainability, Narotam bridges business acumen with ecological responsibility to drive impactful, long-term change.

Our Farm

Nestled in Kutushol, East Singhbhum, Jharkhand, our 12-acre farm is built on the principles of sustainability. We cultivate Moringa through organic practices, producing chemical-free, nutrient-rich food while also restoring soil health and biodiversity.

Within just one year, we have successfully planted around 40,000 Moringa trees and aim to expand to over 500,000 trees in the next five years in partnership with local farmers.

Alongside cultivation, we are committed to empowering communities with sustainable farming knowledge and are planning to serve as a market aggregator to ensure fair and transparent earnings for farmers.
