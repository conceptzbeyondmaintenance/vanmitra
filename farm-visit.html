Farm Visit

Step into the heart of nature at our regenerative Moringa farm in Village Dhilahara, Chakulia, East Singhbhum (Jharkhand). More than a farm visit, it’s an experience of living sustainability—where soil, plants, and people thrive together in harmony.

  • Stroll through vibrant moringa fields and see how the “Miracle Tree” flourishes under regenerative, chemical-free farming practices.
  • Trace the journey from seed to superfood, discovering how purity at the farm transforms into wellness on your plate.
  • Meet the farmers behind the mission—the true changemakers—learning how sustainable agriculture empowers local communities.
  • Reconnect with nature’s rhythm amidst fresh air, green landscapes, and authentic rural hospitality.

Whether you’re a wellness seeker, student, researcher, or conscious traveler, VanMitra Farm promises inspiration, learning, and a heartfelt connection with the land.
